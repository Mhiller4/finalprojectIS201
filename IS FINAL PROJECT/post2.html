Week 2:Tahitians > Einstein

Okay, okay Guys, super super cool fact
Tahitians and Most Polynesians beat Einstein to one of his Philosophy

Sent from Dallas Texas on August 14, 2021

Okay, okay Guys, super super cool fact Tahitians and Most Polynesians beat Einstein to one of his Philosophy, Einstein had this idea that Time moves relative to an observer. Meaning we can only see the past and we move backwards through time. Go check it out, it is a really cool theory. Essentially we only see the past and can't see the future. Now! In Tahitian "I mu'a" means the past and "i muri" means the future.
Now also those same words mean in front, and behind in Tahitian. Leading to a basic motto of, "You cannot predict the future, but you can 1000% read the past, so learn from the past to prepare for the future". Tahitian was around way before Einstein.

So yeah, Tahitians beat Albert Einstein. Boom.
