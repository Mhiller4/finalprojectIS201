Week 3:Dieu est dans les Détails

Eaha To'oe Huru???? Comment ca va? Another week has passed in the MTC, and let me tell ya. This week was the fastest, most packed, and best week so far. I'll break down how this week started.

Sent from Dallas Texas on August 21, 2021

I'll break down how this week started. On monday morning Kiana and I were working out in the morning together when bam. FULL RAINBOW was across the sky, if that doesn't tell you how the week is about to go I don't know what will! But here in Texas we've had rain almost every day. A definite highlight of the week was when it was crazy stormy and my Dog was scared. Oakley (my dog) came and jumped into my arms, in the middle of a MTC lesson!! She sat and the camera cut her off so I don't think anyone saw her, but it was awesome!

She put her face on my notebook so I did not get that many notes out of that class haha! This week started our first week in French Immersion! So starting Tuesday we've been taught fully in French! Which is crazy.

We answer in French and are taught French grammar, but in French!

The rate we learn french in the MTC is incredible, I truly believe I have already surpassed my two years of Spanish.

Teaching Others Appointments

Elder Smith and I had a great chance to teach and hold a discussion for the first time in French yesterday. These are called a "Helping Others Activity" or a TRC. They're essentially opportunities we have to meet with others and share messages of hope and joy with them!

The French one went so well!! Of course there were things I couldn't understand from her, and there were some things Elder Smith and I couldn't communicate. However the discussion went really well, and I think we all came out of it feeling uplifted and more optimistic.

I was so lucky, some of her favorite hobbies were repelling and being in the mountains, so I was able to connect with her in French because I knew those words. And I am grateful Elder Smith and I planned out things together so we never had any awkward places.

Spiritual Thought

So my subject line is "Dieu est dans les détails" which means God is in the Details. God is in the details of your life. I had a cool experience this week in understanding that God cares for each of us.

I found myself getting frustrated about a lot of things I couldn't control. Passports,visa delays, the language, time, etc. I talked with my instructor about some of these concerns and she gave me this amazing insight "God's plan for us is way better than any plan we have for ourselves. "

I felt so reassured with that, and the next day so many of these concerns and insecurities got solved! God cares so much about the welfare of his children.

And while there are a lot of things we cannot control or we don't know why they happen to us, I know God is in our corner and wants what is best for us.

His plan for each of us is going to be so much more fulfilling, edifying, and promising than we can imagine.

Yes there is hardship, yes life is hard, but the way we go through life doesn't have to be.

Conflict is inevitable but contention is a choice.
